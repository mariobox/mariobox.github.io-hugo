Madrid

Madrid is part of my short list of places to visit again. Here are some pictures:


Churreria San Gines. Possibly the best churros in Madrid. Served with thick hot chocolate. Worth every calory.



The Gourmet Experience: great gourmet food court in the 9th floor of El Corte Ingles of Gran Via.



The Crystal Palace, at Parque del Buen Retiro.



Go to Mercado San Miguel, next to Plaza Mayor, for mid-morning tapas and cava.



Exhibit at Museo Reina Sofia.



AVE high speed train.



Jamon Iberico de Bellota. Quite a treat.



Taking a shot at rowing, at Parque del Buen Retiro.



Madrid's Plaza Mayor.



Cambio de Guardia at Palacio Real. Happens every Wednesday (except July, August and September) at 11:00am. Worth it.



Although technically not Madrid, Toledo is only about an hour away and makes a great day trip. Pictured: the Alcazar.
